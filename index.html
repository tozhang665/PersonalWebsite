About Me

I am a recent graduate from San Francisco State University with a bachelor's in Mechanical Engineering and a double minor in Computer Science and Computing Applications.

From a young age, I have always gravitated towards puzzles as well as science. Whether it was solving Rubic's Cubes or taking apart random electronics in the house, I have always been a fast learner and loved learning about how things worked and how almost everything can be explained using math or logic. My Engineering background has allowed me to immerse myself in being able to explore how a lot of phenomenon worked. Due to my experience with Computer Science in college, I noticed I was most excited/euphoric when I was going through a difficult project. Therefore, I decided to pursue a career in Software Engineering.

As a full stack software engineer, I have been able to create projects using Ruby on Rails as well as the MERN stack. I have also used numerous APIs to add functionality to my websites such as Google Maps API, Google Geocoding API as well as Edamam Nutrition API

TagAlongs

TagAlongs is a Volunteer based Buddy system application that allows users to request companionship for their daily routes or activities. Volunteers contribute by accepting TagAlongs in their area, meeting with the user, and providing a safe enviroment for the user to travel in.

Amazin'Zone

Amazin-Zone is a full stack web application that is inspired by the popular online shopping platform Amazon. Users can browse some of the latest items offered as well as Create/Read/Update/Delete reviews on the items listed. Besides creating reviews, Users can also add and delete items into their cart. When the user is ready, They can also Checkout and see the total price of their cart!
